## 1. üöÄ Executive Summary

**EcoBot** is a conceptual design project focused on developing autonomous, solar-powered robotic solutions for multi-functional environmental maintenance in public urban spaces. The project aims to provide sustainable, automated solutions that contribute significantly to the cleanliness, sanitation, and overall quality of life in smart cities.

### Vision Statement

To lead the way in providing cost-effective and ecologically friendly automated solutions to municipal bodies, targeting unprecedented reductions in urban pollution and waste levels.

---

## 2. üéØ Core Functionality & Deliverables

The EcoBot system is designed to execute the following mission-critical tasks efficiently:

* **Automated Waste Collection and Sorting:** Utilizing advanced sensor technology to detect, collect, and preliminarily sort refuse in streets and public parks.
* **Programmed Surface Sterilization:** Implementing efficient sterilization techniques to sanitize shared pedestrian areas and surfaces, enhancing public health.
* **Intelligent Landscape Maintenance:** Performing precise, automated pruning and maintenance of roadside trees and shrubs.
